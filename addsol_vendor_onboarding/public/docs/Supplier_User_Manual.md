# Supplier User Manual

## Purpose

This guide explains how a supplier should complete onboarding, submit documents, and resolve validation issues.

## Before You Start

You should have received:
- Login credentials on your registered email
- Onboarding request from the company

If you did not receive credentials, contact the Purchase Manager.

## Onboarding Form Link

Use this form link to submit onboarding details:

- **Vendor Onboarding Form:** `/vendor-onboarding-form`

If your company shared a full domain URL, use that exact URL with the same route.

## Step 1: Login

1. Open the login page.
2. Sign in using supplied email and password.
3. Go to supplier portal/onboarding page.

## Step 2: Open Your Onboarding Record

1. Locate your onboarding request.
2. Confirm status is `Pending Submission` or `Validation Failed`.
3. Open record for editing.

## Step 3: Fill Required Details Carefully

Enter accurate values exactly as per official records:
- GSTN
- PAN
- CIN/LLPIN (if applicable)
- Bank Account Number
- IFSC Code
- Udyam Registration Number (if applicable)
- Phone number and email

Important:
- Avoid extra spaces.
- Use valid formats.
- Ensure bank details are current and active.

## Step 4: Upload Required Documents

Upload clear and readable files:
- GSTN Certificate
- PAN Card
- Cancelled Cheque / Bank Proof

Optional uploads (if applicable):
- CIN/LLPIN Certificate
- Udyam Certificate
- Company Logo

Tips:
- Upload latest documents only.
- If re-uploading, upload corrected document and save again.

## Step 5: Submit for Verification

1. Submit details.
2. Status changes to `Data Submitted` / `In Progress`.
3. Wait while checks are performed.

## Step 6: Track Status

Possible outcomes:
- `Validation Successful`: waiting for Purchase Manager approval.
- `Validation Failed`: one or more checks failed.
- `Approved`: onboarding complete.

## Step 7: If Validation Failed

1. Open onboarding record and review message.
2. Correct incorrect details.
3. Re-upload supporting document (if needed).
4. Submit again.

Common reasons:
- GSTN/PAN mismatch with provider records
- incorrect IFSC/account combination
- invalid format for CIN/Udyam

## Step 8: After Approval

When approved:
- Supplier account is enabled.
- You can continue business transactions as per company process.

## Support and Escalation

Contact Purchase Manager when:
- you cannot login
- status is stuck for long time
- you believe submitted details are correct but still failing

Share these details in your support request:
- onboarding record ID
- submission date/time
- exact issue screenshot
